Welcome to the City Lighthouse Church

City Lighthouse Church is a vibrant, Christ-centered community located in the heart of Coventry. Established in 2012, we are committed to shining the light of Jesus in our city and beyond. Whether you're new to faith, seeking a place to grow, or looking for a community to belong to — you are warmly welcome here.

Our church is built on strong Biblical teaching, Spirit-filled worship, and genuine fellowship. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of family, and the calling to serve our neighborhoods with love and compassion.

Come and be part of what God is doing at City Lighthouse Church — there's a place for you here!
