About Us

City Lighthouse Community Church is a vibrant Pentecostal Christian church established in 2022, serving the Tamil-speaking community from all nations. Our mission is to spread the love of Jesus Christ and empower believers through the infilling and guidance of the Holy Spirit. As a welcoming family rooted in biblical truth, we focus on nurturing faith, hope, and joyful service in every member. We celebrate the Pentecostal experience of spiritual gifts, prayer, and revival, equipping individuals to live victorious Christian lives and impact their communities. Whether you are new to faith or looking for a spirit-filled church home, City Lighthouse Community Church invites you to grow in the grace and power of God with us. Learn More

Our Ministries

Worship Ministry

Experience God’s presence through passionate praise and worship. Our worship team leads the congregation into a deeper connection with God during every service. With heartfelt songs, powerful lyrics, and Spirit-led worship, we create an atmosphere where lives are transformed, burdens are lifted, and God’s name is glorified. Whether you're a singer, musician, or someone who simply loves to worship, there’s a place for you here to grow and serve in the presence of the Lord.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to live boldly for Christ. Our Youth Ministry is a vibrant space where teens and young adults can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose. Through engaging activities, powerful discipleship, worship nights, and real conversations about life and faith, we equip the next generation to stand firm in Christ and impact their world with truth and love.

Explore More →

Outreach Ministry

Taking the love of Christ beyond the church walls. Our Outreach Ministry is dedicated to serving others with compassion and purpose. Whether it’s food drives, street outreach, visiting the vulnerable, or hosting community events, we’re committed to being the hands and feet of Jesus in our local community—sharing hope, meeting needs, and making a real difference in people's lives.

Children’s Ministry

Fun, safe, and Christ-centered. Our Children’s Ministry creates an engaging environment where kids can learn about Jesus in a way that’s fun, interactive, and easy to understand. Through Bible stories, worship, games, and creative activities, we nurture a foundation of faith that will grow with them for life..
