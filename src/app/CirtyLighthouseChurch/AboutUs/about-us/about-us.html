About City Lighthouse Church

City Lighthouse Community Church is a vibrant Pentecostal church established in 2022, serving the Tamil-speaking community worldwide. Rooted in the power of the Gospel and led by the Holy Spirit, we are a family united by faith and love.

Our mission is to glorify God by spreading Jesus’ love and empowering believers through Spirit baptism, prayer, worship, and spiritual gifts.

We believe everyone is uniquely created by God and called to walk in purpose. Whether exploring faith or looking for a church family, you are welcome here.

Our Commitments

  • Proclaiming the Gospel boldly
  • Raising disciples who walk in holiness and love
  • Encouraging growth through prayer and fellowship
  • Supporting families and the next generation
  • Reaching the lost with compassion

Join us for joyful worship, heartfelt prayer, and Bible-based preaching that addresses real life. Encounter God’s presence and grow in His grace and power.
