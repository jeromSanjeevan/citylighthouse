About City Lighthouse Church

City Lighthouse Church is a Spirit-filled, Christ-centered community located in Coventry, UK.

Founded in 2012, our mission is to bring the light of Christ to the city and beyond. We are a family-oriented church that believes in powerful worship, solid biblical teaching, and serving our local community with love and compassion.

Our vision is to be a lighthouse of hope, faith, and transformation. Join us and be part of what God is doing!
